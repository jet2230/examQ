📜 How to Play

  • Host: Chooses a secret word/phrase. Spaces become slashes (/).
  • Players: Take turns guessing letters or numbers (if enabled).
  • Winning: Reveal the full word before the hangman is complete!
  • Winner becomes Host: The person who makes the final guess sets the next word.
  • Clues: Guests can request 1 clue per game. The host can send multiple hints.
