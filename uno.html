📜 How to Play

  • Starting: At least 2 players are required to begin.
  • Gameplay: Match cards by color or value. Play continues until only one person is left with cards!
  • CALL UNO: When you have exactly 1 card left, you MUST click the CALL UNO! button before playing your final card.
  • Disputes: If a player finishes without calling UNO, any other player can click Dispute Win to force them back into the game with 2 penalty cards.
  • Host: The first person to clear their hand (the winner) becomes the host for the next game.
